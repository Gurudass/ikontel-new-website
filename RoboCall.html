What is RoboCall?

A robocall is an automated phone call that uses prerecorded messages to reach recipients. Unlike live calls, robocalls are generated by machines and are often used for telemarketing, surveys, or automated notifications. They can efficiently deliver a consistent message to a large number of people, but they can also be perceived as intrusive or annoying if not used appropriately.

Robocalls are commonly employed by businesses for routine announcements, appointment reminders, or customer outreach.

Is RoboCall secure?

How can I identify if a call is a robo-call?

Robo calls often have pre-recorded messages, lack interactive responses, and may come from unfamiliar numbers. If you receive a call that seems automated or offers options to press numbers, it may be a robo-call.
